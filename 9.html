Mist Breathing Mist Breathing Hashira, Muichiro Tokito in Demon Slayer Muichiro Tokito is the youngest of the Hashira and a descendant of the Upper Rank One demon Kokushibo. He accomplished Hashira status after only two months of training. Mist Breathing copies how the mists obscure and allow for swift strikes after a cloud of confusion is created. There are six forms of Mist Breathing, plus a seventh developed by Muichiro. Mist Breathing is a sub-style of Wind Breathing. The seventh form, Obscuring Clouds, is the strongest use of Mist Breathing because it both disorients, defends with an envelope of mist, and delivers strikes in the blink of an eye.
