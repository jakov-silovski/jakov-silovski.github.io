Insect Breathing Shinobu sticking a sword to her chest in Demon Slayer A derivative of Water & Flower Breathing, Shinobu Kocho uses Insect Breathing. Shinobu created this technique to mimic lethal stings and movements of insects. Her sword is notoriously thin with a needle tip to replicate the stinger function to inject Wisteria poison into demons. There are four techniques called Butterfly Dance, Dance of the Bee Sting, Dance of the Dragonfly, and Dance of the Centipede. Shinobu matches the movements of the butterfly, dragonfly, and centipede, as well as the sting and speed of the bee, to behead her demon adversaries.
