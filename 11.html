Flame Breathing Kengoku surrounded by fire in Demon Slayer One of the most renowned Hashira and a fan-favorite Demon Slayer character, Kyojuro Rengoku, was the primary user of Flame Breathing. Fans were able to see just how skilled he was in the recent Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba: Mugen Train movie. Flame Breathing has nine techniques, the ninth being a specialty of Demon Slayer's Rengoku family. Flame Breathing uses the movement of flames and the user's abilities to enact intense, burning strikes. The key to Flame Breathing is visualizing the manipulation of the flames. Flame Breathing allowed for the creation of Love Breathing, developed by another Hashira, Mitsuri Kanroji, to better use her unique muscles.
